Sushi

Salmon Sushi

Recipe:

  • Cook short-grain rice and season with vinegar.
  • Slice fresh salmon thinly.
  • Shape rice into small balls and place salmon on top.

Fun Fact: Sushi was first made as a way to preserve fish in rice before becoming the fresh dish we know today!
